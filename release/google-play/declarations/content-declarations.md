## UGC release gate

The release owner must confirm that users can report inappropriate content or behavior, operators can moderate/remove it, terms prohibit abuse, and a monitored support/escalation path exists. Store submission must stop if the release candidate or operations process does not satisfy the applicable Google Play user-generated-content policy.
